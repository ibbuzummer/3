About Us

Chrysalis High - Yelahanka New Town is located in the heart of Yelahanka, just 500 meters from Yelahanka Police Station & is affiliated with CBSE. A protected space to grow and develop, to unfold and explore, a foothold to grip and fly …

The school campus is spread over a sprawling 2-acre area with lots of facilities for outdoor activities that include a semi-Olympic size swimming pool, skating rink, basketball court, cricket pitch and a comfortable play area for tiny tots.

Our Vision

Chrysalis is a journey for the nascent. Charles Darwin explains the school as the “proper place on which to become attached and undergo the final metamorphosis.”

In this golden age of childhood, children need a protected place which shelters the vulnerable and allows them the space and the freedom to transform themselves to their full glory.

Children are innocent beings, to be treated with utmost care and responsibility till the time they are strong enough to soar.

At Chrysalis High, this is exactly what we help the children do. Soar .. !

Why Chrysalis ?

To help our children excel by understanding them, guiding them on the right path and strengthening their self belief.

My School

Facilities :

Class room

All classrooms are well lit and ventilated. The elevation of the building is kept vibrant & bright to make it look welcoming for all our children.

Library & Labs

Our Resource center is equipped with the latest books, periodicals & research reports. Resource center has broadband internet access. Computer-aided learning is an integral part of Chrysalis High, especially higher classes.

Chrysalis High has fully equipped computer labs with provision for a separate computer for each child. Computer labs have broadband internet access which is shared with the children under supervision. Chrysalis High has labs for Biology, Chemistry, Physics & Maths for practical learning of the concepts. All labs are designed as per the specifications & requirement of the CBSE board.

Cafeteria

In order to inculcate good eating habits and enhance bonding between a teacher and students, we encourage students and teacher of each grade to sit together and eat. Chrysalis High serves only vegetarian meals inside the premises. Children are free to bring non-vegetarian food from home.

Indoor CHAMPS

Chrysalis High has separate demarcated areas for Western Music, Contemporary Dance, Theatre, Gymnastics, Chess & Yoga. All the Performing Arts are taught to the children by experienced coaches who specialise in that activity.

Outdoor CHAMPS

With an equal emphasis on “housing a healthy mind in a healthy body,” Chrysalis High strives to meet the need of overall development of the child by providing a Semi-Olympic size swimming pool, basketball court & a cricket pitch and a skating rink.

Performing Arts CHAMPS

In order to enhance and develop the creativity of our children, we have created a robust Performing Arts CHAMPS Program.

Transport Facilities

Transportation at Chrysalis High is planned meticulously so that the children travel the least amount of time. Each bus has a maid carrying a mobile phone. Transportation routes are communicated to parents well in advance. Chrysalis High provides transportation to all its students within a 15 km radius of the school. Parents seeking transportation beyond 15 km can contact the transportation desk at the school front office, mail us or call us at the school contact numbers.

   

PUPA

  • PUPA which expands to Prior Understanding of Pupil Traits is our forte.
  • Through PUPA we ensure that there is complete understanding of child’s personality by everyone involved to support the child better.
  • Our teachers spend time with each parent individually in an effort to know the child, little things that will help the teacher handle the child in a way which is optimal to his/ her learning.
  • Over the months, the teacher will add her observations and record anecdotes that shape your child.

STE(A)M (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, & Math)

  • As the future work force will focus on Data Science, Automation and Technology – Coding, Advanced Robotics, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Chrysalis High focuses on building these concepts into our kids minds
  • Using Imported & Latest Science Kits (from countries leading in the usage of Educational Technology, concepts are taught by STE(A)M Facilitators
  • Chrysalis High focuses on Programming Languages used in Automation like Scratch / MakeBlock, Python, Java, C. These Programming Languages are used in coding the Robots thereby manifesting the ideas onto the Physical World by actually seeing the codes running in Robots
  • Building robots for specific problems like measuring the climate, sensing the obstacles, etc are also carried by the students which help them in learning the engineering design process.
  • Creating art using the robots built by the students (Spirograph, Abstract Art) enables the kids understand th working of the robots and develop technical skills ..

In-door, Out-door and Performing Arts CHAMPS

  • Children can select activities like Swimming, Skating, Basketball, Gymnastics, Yoga, Contemporary Dance, Western Music, Theatre and Chess
  • Each of the activities is taught to the child by a coach who specializes in that field. The children are observed closely so as to understand & hone their talent in any of the Sports or Performing Arts.
  • If any child wishes to pursue any of the activities as his/her career, the child is counseled on the same and helped/ guided in exploring options available in the country & globally.

HOLD MY HAND Program

  • Individual Support Program is one of the key differentiators of Chrysalis High. While we truly believe in celebrating excellence, we also believe that at one point or another, every child needs support
  • A thorough process of identification of skill gaps, target based program and strategy based remediation has helped many children recover their academic losses and integrate into the classroom with confidence and motivation.
